We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from KingsMan. If for any reason you are not, we offer a hassle-free returns and exchange policy. Please read the information below to learn more about our policy.

Cancellations

If you need to cancel an order, please call our customer service team at (020) 7604 5949 as soon as possible. Although we cannot guarantee we can cancel your order before it is dispatched, we will do our best to assist.

Exchanges

We offer free exchanges up to 28 days after your order has been delivered. To initiate an exchange, please click the button below and follow the instructions.

Returns

We offer 2 options when it comes to returning an item for a refund. We can either refund your order to your original payment method, or we can issue a refund to store credit.

Refund to your original payment method

Refund to store credit

Eligible for returns booked up to 28 days after your order has been delivered, free of charge using a prepaid UPS label generated at point of booking your return. Credit expires after 6 months of issue.

Return Terms & Conditions

All returns, whether for exchange or refund, go through a strict quality-control assessment once they have arrived back at our warehouse. All goods must be returned in original condition, with all labels and security tags intact. Any items returned that have been worn or have had the tags removed, will be rejected. Rejected returns will either be postage back to you at your expense, or donated to charity after 14 days.
